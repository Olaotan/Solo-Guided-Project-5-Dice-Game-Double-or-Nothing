How To Play

Welcome to this simple game of luck and strategy.

First to get 20 scores wins.
Simply roll the dice, or up the ante by rolling the Double or Nothing dice.

Once you have up to 5 points, you can crank it up even further with the Triple Dice...but there's a catch! as it could also wipe out all your scores.
